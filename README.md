## What About Extra Costs?

Worth knowing: your subscription covers the platform, but usage-based services cost extra. This includes:

- **LC Phone System**: ~$1.15/month per local number, $2/month for toll-free; per-minute call charges apply
- **LC Email**: $0.675 per 1,000 emails sent (cheaper than most standalone SMTP providers)
- **AI features, workflow executions, and other premium add-ons**: charged separately via a wallet system

On the Unlimited and Agency Pro plans, you can rebill these usage costs to clients — at cost or with a markup — which turns a variable cost into a revenue stream.
